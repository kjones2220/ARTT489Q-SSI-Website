The Student Success Initiative is a triade of organizations.

Although all three organizations serve different purposes, whether it be event planning, digital media, or community service, they all have the same overarching mission.

We build student-led programs that empower the Black student population at the University of Maryland, College Park academically, professionally, and financially.

SSI's goal is to increase the graduation and retention rates of Black students at the University of Maryland.

Our Organizations

SSLC

The Student Success Leadership Council hosts four main events to connect alumni, students, staff/faculty, and employers. These four events are FreshCon, NetNite, Brunch For Success, and the Gift of Giving Gala.

UMSuccess

UMSuccess is the digital home for Black students at the university. This is where you can find a spotlight on members within our community through social media campaigns like Souls of UMD or attend a branding and entrepreneurship workshop called Brand Your Shell.

STEP

STEP is the original organization out of the triade. STEP was initially created in order to specifically support the Black male community due to the disparities between their graduation and retention rates and the entire university. They have since hosted numerous social and community service events.
